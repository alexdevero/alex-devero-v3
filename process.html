My Process

Learn

Exceptional websites start by understanding your requirements. I will ask you questions to understand your image, goals, budget, target markets, content requirements, reference sites, materials and desired outcomes. Basically, I will cover everything you know and want at this point.

Plan

Once I have learned what you need, it’s time to start planning how we are going to make it happen. I will map out your sites architecture, defining sections and pages including their flow and relationship to each other. I will create a functional specification—a document that sets out in detail the things your website must do, and how it will do them.

Design

I will get my creative ideas flowing which results in us sketching and mocking-up your site. Choices about layout, type size and colour palettes are tested within a number of ideas-I will identify what works best in order to communicate your website’s goals. Then, I will review the mock ups with you and refine/revise accordingly until you will be satisfied.

Build

Once your killer design is signed off I put your site into development. I will build a working system that mimics the final design with clean and semantic code. Your site is then deployed to a staging environment where I will rigorously test its functionality and fix any bugs before handing the system over to you for testing.

Launch

Once everyone is satisfied I will put your website into a launch-ready state. Your website will require content. I will work with you to ensure you are unstoppable when it comes to using and populating your new website. When you’ve finally perfected the site, it’s time to release it to the public!
