Discovery

Exceptional design start by understanding your requirements. I will ask you questions to understand your image, goals, budget, target markets, content requirements, reference sites, materials and desired outcomes. Basically, I will cover everything you know and want at this point so I can create perfect, tailored, design.

Planning

Once I have learned what you need, it's time to start planning how we are going to make it happen. I will map out all the parts of the design and create architecture that will work as building foundations in the next phases. I will also create a functional specification—a document that sets out in detail all the things that must be done and how to get them done.

Design

I will get my creative ideas flowing by sketching and mocking-up different ideas of your design. Choices about design direction, layout, typography, content and color palettes are tested by prototyping a number of ideas. I will identify what works best in order to communicate your goals. Then, I will review the mockups with you and refine until you will be satisfied.

Building

Once your killer design is signed off I put your product into development. I will build a working system that will perfectly correspond to the final design idea. Your product is then deployed to a staging environment where I will rigorously test its functionality and fix any bugs. Then, I will hand the system over to you for final testing.

Evaluation

Evaluation phase is the last part of my design process. I will systematically test the result of my work to see if it achieves your business and project goals. Evaluation will help us find potential weaknesses of my design. Then, I'll use this feedback to fix all of these weaknesses and any other gaps. Result will be flawless and your satisfaction.
