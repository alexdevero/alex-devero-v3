I'm Alex Devero

A designer who loves code.

I am a multidisciplinary designer, developer & entrepreneur from Prague, Czech Republic. I have been designing, developing websites, building interactive experiences and creating brands for over seven years. I'm a fan and promoter of structure and simplicity in both design and code. Organization and consistency are very important to me.

A few of my strengths are UI/UX design, web design, web development (front-end), typography, app design, branding, corporate identity design, graphic design, logo design, print design and also writing. However, my shtick is web design, especially front-end development.

I find inspiration in nature, architecture, photography, stores, magazines and literature. When I'm not working, you can find me at library, shopping center, exploring new places on the Internet or walking in the streets. In my spare time, I like to spend my time doing calisthenics, practising yoga, meditating, reading interesting books or enjoying a cup of tea, or coffee.

My personality in 5 points:

  • 1. Passionate (I absolutely love what I do.)
  • 2. Enthusiastic (I have insatiable desire to learn.)
  • 3. Sceptical (I think critically.)
  • 4. Focused (I have 'get sh*t done' mindset.)
  • 5. Relentless (I will never stop or give up.)
